ABOUT THE DESIGNER

Christine Hyun Mi Nielsen

is the founder and Creative Director of Hyun Mi Nielsen.

She is Danish, born in South Korea.

Educated in Copenhagen and London where she

studied at the Royal College of Art and Design.

She lives and works in Paris.

Before founding Hyun Mi Nielsen in July 2016,

She has worked as Directrice de Studio PAP Femme at Balenciaga and Givenchy in Paris and Head of Womenswear Design at Alexander McQueen in London.
